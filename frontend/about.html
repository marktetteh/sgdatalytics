ABOUT SG DATALYTICS

We are building Ghana's most trusted data warehouse — curating, verifying, and delivering high-quality datasets that power research, business, and policy decisions.

Our mission

SG Datalytics exists to close Ghana's data gap. Too many researchers, businesses, and policymakers struggle to find reliable, structured, and up-to-date data about Ghana's economy, demographics, and key sectors. We solve that by collecting, cleaning, and packaging data into ready-to-use formats — so decision-makers can focus on analysis, not data wrangling.

What we do

We operate a data marketplace that spans six core sectors: education, business and SMEs, health and demographics, sustainability, agriculture, and the economy. Our team continuously collects data from verified sources, structures it into clean formats (CSV, Excel, JSON, SPSS, Shapefile), and makes it available through affordable subscription plans.

Every dataset goes through a multi-stage quality process — from source verification and cleaning to documentation and formatting — before it appears in our catalog. For details, see our Methodology page.

Who we serve

Our users include MPhil and PhD researchers working on dissertations, business analysts benchmarking Ghanaian markets, government agencies making policy decisions, NGOs tracking development indicators, journalists reporting on national trends, and students building analytical skills with real-world data.

SG Datalytics is based in Ashalley Botwe New Town, Accra, Ghana. We work with local data sources, field enumerators, and institutional partners across all regions of Ghana to ensure our datasets reflect the full picture of the country.
